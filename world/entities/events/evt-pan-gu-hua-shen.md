# 盘古化身 Pangu's Transformation

天地稳固之后，盘古耗尽了所有的力量。他倒下了。他的左眼化为太阳，右眼化为月亮，血液化为江河，骨骼化为山脉，毛发化为草木。他的最后一口气，化为了风。

After heaven and earth were stable, Pangu exhausted all his strength. He fell. His left eye became the sun, his right eye became the moon, his blood became rivers, his bones became mountains, his hair became vegetation. His last breath became the wind.
