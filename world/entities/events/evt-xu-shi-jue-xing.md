# 叙事觉醒 The Narrative Awakening

当虚无将他的研究成果公之于众时，整个世界陷入了前所未有的恐慌。人们开始质疑自己的每一个决定是否是"自由意志"，还是"剧本"的安排。一些人试图做出"不合逻辑"的行为来证明自由意志的存在，但他们发现，即使是"反叛"也在叙事的预期之中。

When Xuwu made his research public, the entire world fell into unprecedented panic. People began to question whether their every decision was "free will" or an arrangement of the "script." Some tried to perform "illogical" actions to prove the existence of free will, but they discovered that even "rebellion" was within the narrative's expectations.
