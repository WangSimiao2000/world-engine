# 不周山 Mount Buzhou

天地初分之时，盘古的脊骨化为一座通天巨山——不周山。它是天地之间唯一的支柱，山顶触及天穹，山根深入地底。传说若此山崩塌，天将倾覆，地将陷落。

When heaven and earth first separated, Pangu's spine transformed into a mountain that reached the sky — Mount Buzhou. It is the sole pillar between heaven and earth, its peak touching the firmament, its roots reaching deep underground. Legend says that if this mountain collapses, heaven will tilt and earth will sink.
