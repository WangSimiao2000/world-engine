# 盘古斧 Pangu's Axe

混沌之中自然凝聚而成的神器。它不是被铸造的，而是与盘古一同从虚无中诞生。斧刃可以切割一切——包括"有"与"无"的边界。开天辟地之后，此斧碎裂为无数碎片，散落于天地之间。

A divine artifact that naturally coalesced within chaos. It was not forged, but born from the void alongside Pangu. Its blade can cut through anything — including the boundary between existence and non-existence. After the separation of heaven and earth, the axe shattered into countless fragments scattered across the world.
