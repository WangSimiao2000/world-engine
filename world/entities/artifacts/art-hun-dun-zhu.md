# 混沌珠 Chaos Pearl

开天辟地之后，并非所有混沌都被分化。一小团最纯粹的混沌之力凝聚成珠，悬浮于天地之间。它既不属于天，也不属于地，是这个世界中唯一保留着"创世之前"记忆的存在。

After the separation of heaven and earth, not all chaos was differentiated. A small mass of the purest chaotic power condensed into a pearl, suspended between heaven and earth. It belongs neither to heaven nor to earth — it is the only existence in this world that retains the memory of "before creation."
