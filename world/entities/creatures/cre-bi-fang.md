# 毕方 Bifang

形如鹤，一足，赤色。它是天地初分时从地火中诞生的神鸟。毕方所过之处，草木自燃，但这并非毁灭——在混沌纪，火是净化的力量，是旧世界残余被清除的方式。

Shaped like a crane, one-legged, crimson in color. It is a divine bird born from the earth's fire when heaven and earth first separated. Wherever Bifang passes, vegetation spontaneously ignites — but this is not destruction. In the Chaos Epoch, fire is the power of purification, the way remnants of the old world are cleared away.
