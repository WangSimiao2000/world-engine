# 烛阴兽 Zhuyin Beast

烛阴掌管昼夜之时，偶有鳞片脱落。这些鳞片落入幽暗之地，吸收了黑暗的力量，化为一种能够吞噬光明的怪兽。它们栖息在永远照不到光的角落，是这个世界最早的掠食者。

When Zhuyin governed the alternation of day and night, scales occasionally fell from his body. These scales fell into dark places, absorbed the power of darkness, and transformed into monsters capable of devouring light. They dwell in corners where light never reaches — the world's earliest predators.
