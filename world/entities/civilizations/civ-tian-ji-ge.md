# 天机阁 The Tianji Pavilion

当世界经历了六个纪元的演化之后，天机阁的学者们开始注意到一个令人不安的规律：所有重大事件的发生模式，都符合某种"叙事结构"。英雄总是在最需要的时候出现，灾难总是在文明最繁盛时降临，而每一次"拯救"都恰好在最后一刻完成。

这不是巧合。这是被设计的。

After the world had evolved through six epochs, scholars of the Tianji Pavilion began to notice a disturbing pattern: the occurrence pattern of all major events conformed to a certain "narrative structure." Heroes always appeared when most needed, disasters always struck at the peak of civilization, and every "salvation" was completed at precisely the last moment.

This was not coincidence. This was designed.
