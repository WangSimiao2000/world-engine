# 混沌遗民 Remnants of Chaos

并非所有混沌中的存在都接受了"被分化"的命运。一些古老的意志拒绝成为天或地、阴或阳，它们保持着混沌的本来面目，游离于新世界的秩序之外。后世称它们为"混沌遗民"。它们是世界中最古老的异类，也是所有"不可理解之物"的源头。

Not all beings within chaos accepted the fate of "being differentiated." Some ancient wills refused to become heaven or earth, yin or yang. They maintained the original form of chaos, existing outside the order of the new world. Later ages called them "Remnants of Chaos." They are the oldest outsiders in the world, and the source of all "incomprehensible things."
