# 元灵族 The Yuanling

混沌被劈开之后，并非所有混沌之力都化为了物质。一部分混沌之力保留了"意识"的特质，形成了最初的生命形态——元灵。它们没有实体，是纯粹的意识流，漂浮在天地之间。随着世界逐渐物质化，元灵族逐渐消散，但它们的意识碎片融入了后世万物之中。

After chaos was split, not all chaotic power transformed into matter. A portion retained the quality of "consciousness," forming the earliest life form — the Yuanling. They had no physical bodies, existing as pure streams of consciousness drifting between heaven and earth. As the world gradually materialized, the Yuanling faded, but fragments of their consciousness merged into all things of later ages.
