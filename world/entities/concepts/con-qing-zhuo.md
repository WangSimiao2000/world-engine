# 清浊二气 The Two Qi

盘古劈开混沌的那一刻，混沌分化为两种截然不同的力量：清气与浊气。清气轻盈上升，凝聚为天穹；浊气沉重下降，凝固为大地。这是世界最基本的二元结构，也是后世一切"阴阳"思想的源头。

The moment Pangu split chaos, it differentiated into two fundamentally different forces: clear qi and turbid qi. The clear qi, light and buoyant, rose and condensed into the firmament; the turbid qi, heavy and dense, sank and solidified into the earth. This is the most fundamental binary structure of the world, and the origin of all later "yin-yang" thought.
