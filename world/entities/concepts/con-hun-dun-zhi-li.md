# 混沌之理 The Principle of Chaos

在天地分离之前，混沌并非"无序"，而是一种超越了有序与无序之分的状态。后世的哲学家称之为"混沌之理"——认为宇宙的本源状态是一切可能性的叠加，而"创世"本身是一种对可能性的坍缩。

Before the separation of heaven and earth, chaos was not "disorder" but a state that transcended the distinction between order and disorder. Later philosophers called this "The Principle of Chaos" — believing that the original state of the universe was a superposition of all possibilities, and that "creation" itself was a collapse of possibility.
