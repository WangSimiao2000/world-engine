# 叙事律 The Narrative Law

天机阁最核心的发现。他们发现世界的运行并非遵循物理法则，而是遵循"叙事法则"——事件的发生不是因为因果关系，而是因为"故事需要"。每一个英雄的诞生、每一场战争的爆发、每一个文明的兴衰，都是叙事结构的必然展开。

The core discovery of the Tianji Pavilion. They found that the world does not operate according to physical laws, but according to "narrative laws" — events occur not because of causality, but because "the story requires it." Every hero's birth, every war's outbreak, every civilization's rise and fall is the inevitable unfolding of narrative structure.
