# 烛阴 Zhuyin

盘古开天辟地之后，天地之间仍无光暗之分。烛阴自混沌余烬中诞生，身长千里，人面蛇身。他睁眼则天地大明，闭眼则万物入暗。他的呼吸化为四季之风。

After Pangu separated heaven and earth, there was still no distinction between light and dark. Zhuyin was born from the remnants of chaos — a thousand li long, with a human face and serpent body. When he opens his eyes, heaven and earth are illuminated; when he closes them, all things fall into darkness. His breath becomes the winds of the four seasons.
