# 虚无 Xuwu

天机阁的首席学者。他穷尽一生研究世界的运行规律，最终得出了一个令所有人恐惧的结论：这个世界是一个"故事"，而所有人都是"角色"。他试图打破叙事律的束缚，但发现他的"反抗"本身也是叙事的一部分。

The chief scholar of the Tianji Pavilion. He spent his entire life studying the operating laws of the world, ultimately reaching a conclusion that terrified everyone: this world is a "story," and all people are "characters." He tried to break free from the constraints of the Narrative Law, but discovered that his "rebellion" itself was part of the narrative.
