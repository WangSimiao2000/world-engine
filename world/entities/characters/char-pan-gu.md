# 盘古 Pangu

混沌之中沉睡万年的巨神。当他醒来时，挥动巨斧将混沌劈开——清气上升为天，浊气下沉为地。他以身躯撑住天地，日复一日，直到天地稳固。最终，他的身体化为山川河流、日月星辰。

The giant who slumbered for ten thousand years within chaos. When he awoke, he swung his great axe and split chaos apart — the clear qi rose to become heaven, the turbid qi sank to become earth. He held heaven and earth apart with his body, day after day, until they were stable. In the end, his body transformed into mountains, rivers, sun, moon, and stars.
