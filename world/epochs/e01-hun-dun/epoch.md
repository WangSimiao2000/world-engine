# 混沌 — Primordial Chaos

天地未分之时，宇宙是一团无形无质的混沌。没有光，没有暗，没有上，没有下。万物的种子沉睡在这无尽的虚无之中，等待着被唤醒的那一刻。

In the time before heaven and earth were separated, the universe was a formless, shapeless chaos. No light, no dark, no up, no down. The seeds of all things slumbered in this endless void, awaiting the moment of awakening.
