# 元寂 — The Meta Silence

当最后的文明触及世界的边界，他们发现了一个令人战栗的真相：这个世界并非自然演化的产物，而是一个被"叙述"出来的存在。每一次英雄的崛起、每一场战争的爆发、每一个文明的兴衰，都遵循着某种叙事律。

When the final civilization reached the boundaries of the world, they discovered a terrifying truth: this world was not a product of natural evolution, but an existence that had been "narrated" into being. Every hero's rise, every war's outbreak, every civilization's rise and fall — all followed a certain narrative law.
